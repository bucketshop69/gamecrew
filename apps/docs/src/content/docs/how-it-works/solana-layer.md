GameCrew's crypto layer is deliberately small and deliberately playful: the
gifts you win are **real NFTs on Solana devnet**, and that's it. No token, no
trading, no real money.

## Why put gifts on-chain?

Because bragging rights need receipts.

- **Ownership is real.** A lambo you won by calling a 90th-minute goal is
  yours — minted to your wallet, verifiable by anyone.
- **Scarcity is provable.** Rarity means something when the chain can confirm
  it.
- **Money stays out.** Gifts are only ever acquired free inside GameCrew, and
  nothing has a purchase path or cash value. The chain provides ownership,
  not a market.

## How a trophy gets minted

1. You win gifts through the [Playful Economy](/journey/playful-economy/) —
   pre-match gifts, call rewards, pool shares.
2. You choose to retire a gift as a **trophy** — taking it out of play,
   permanently.
3. The app files a claim, and the backend mints the NFT to your wallet on
   devnet using **Metaplex Core** — each trophy is a plain, transferable
   `mpl-core` asset, no collection gymnastics required.
4. Claim status is trackable per claim and per wallet until the mint lands.

Every claim references the **source match event** that earned it — even the
on-chain layer follows the product's honesty rule: everything traces back to
real football.

## Distribution

GameCrew is being submitted to the **Solana dApp Store**, putting a playful,
no-real-money football companion in front of the Solana mobile audience.
