## The story in one line

Most fans already watch football with a phone in hand — but the score app is
passive, the chat is chaos, and the betting apps want your wallet. GameCrew
folds the useful parts into one live match flow: **a match poster, a live
timeline, a stylized broadcast, and a rowdy room of friends** — powered by
TxLINE live data and a playful Solana economy.
