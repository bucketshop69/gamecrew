Football is better with a crowd. Global Chat gives every match **one shared
room** — everyone watching that game, in the same place, reacting to the same
moments.

![Global Chat mid-match — coolness and the gift pool in the header, open call prompts, goal celebrations, and quick reactions along the bottom](/screens/global-chat.png)

## How it works

- **One room per match.** No private rooms, no fragmentation. The whole crowd
  is in it together.
- **Reactions and quick messages** keep the room alive without demanding
  essays — fire off a reaction mid-attack without leaving the match view.
- **The room reacts to the football.** Goals, cards, and VAR drama hit the
  chat as shared moments, because everyone sees them at the same time.
- **Challenges surface in the room.** Pinned challenges and call prompts
  appear right in the flow of chat — this is where the
  [Playful Economy](/journey/playful-economy/) lives.

## Why it matters

The chat is what turns GameCrew from a viewer into a *place*. The timeline
tells you what happened; the room tells you how it felt. And when someone
stakes their Lambo on "goal in the next 5 minutes" — the whole room watches
it settle.
