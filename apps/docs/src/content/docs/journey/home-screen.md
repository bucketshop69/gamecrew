When you open GameCrew, you land on one question:

> **Which match do I want to follow right now?**

No dashboard, no news feed, no bottom tabs. The screen is the match.

![GameCrew home screen — a World Cup match poster with country flags, kickoff time, and the competition label](/screens/home-screen.png)

## The match poster carousel

The main surface is a large **match poster** that takes most of the screen —
embedded into the black app background, not a bordered card. Each poster
shows:

- the two teams (or country flags)
- the score and match minute if live
- the start time if upcoming
- replay status if the match is finished

Swipe sideways to browse matches. Tap a poster to open **Match Detail** — the
screen where Match Pulse, Game View, and Global Chat live.

## Filters

Below the poster, four simple filters change what the carousel shows:

| Filter | What you get |
| --- | --- |
| **Live** | Matches happening right now |
| **Upcoming** | What's kicking off next |
| **Replay** | Finished matches you can replay as if live |
| **Hosted** | Hosted match rooms (coming later) |

## What happens next

Tapping a poster drops you into the match. The first thing you see is the
live story — [Match Pulse](/journey/match-pulse/) — with
[Game View](/journey/game-view/) and [Global Chat](/journey/global-chat/) one
tab away.
