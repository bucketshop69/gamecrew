The playful economy is GameCrew's answer to "betting with friends" — with all
of the fun and none of the money.

> **We hand you a gift before the match, you stake it on what happens next,
> and the real match decides — win and your coolness climbs, lose and the
> gift is gone.**

![The gift popup landing over a match — "We've got some gifts for you. Want to see what you got?" with Claim and Skip for now](/screens/gift-popup.png)

## The loop

1. **A gift lands.** Open a match around kickoff and GameCrew hands you a free
   gift — bananas, dust, maybe a lambo. Every match, fresh ammo. You can never
   be locked out of playing.
2. **A call appears.** The match generates prompts off the live data: *"A goal
   in the first half?"*, *"Who scores next?"*, *"A card in the next 10
   minutes?"* Each call shows the prize up front — stake your bananas, win a
   lambo.
3. **You stake a gift.** Staking is real: lose the call and the gift is gone,
   swallowed by the match's **Gift Pool**.
4. **The real match settles it.** Calls settle from confirmed match events —
   the same data stream that drives the timeline and the board. If VAR wipes
   the goal, the call voids and your stake comes back.
5. **Full time: the pool splits.** The Gift Pool — seeded by GameCrew,
   fattened by every lost stake — splits among everyone who won at least one
   call.

![The match room mid-economy — open call prompts with team picks, coolness and the gift pool in the header, and gift drops landing after goals](/screens/global-chat.png)

## Coolness — the score that only goes up

Winning calls earns **coolness**, GameCrew's status score.

- Coolness **never decreases**. Losses cost you ammo, never status.
- It can't be bought, spent, or cashed out.
- It feeds the leaderboard — you earn coolness, you show off coolness.

## The playful rule

This is the line that keeps GameCrew fun (and keeps it from being a betting
product):

> **Free gifts in, real stakes on real matches, coolness only climbs, zero
> real money.**

- Gifts are **only ever acquired free** — there is no purchase path, no
  deposits, no odds pricing, no cash-out.
- Betting *language* is allowed because every stake is a free gift, never
  money. Real-money mechanics are banned.

## And it's on-chain

Gifts you retire as **trophies** are minted as real NFTs on Solana devnet —
provable, ownable, braggable. See [The Solana Layer](/how-it-works/solana-layer/).
