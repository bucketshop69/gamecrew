GameCrew is a **mobile-first live match companion** for football fans watching
with a phone in hand.

It is not a scoreboard, a news feed, a fantasy app, or a real-money betting
product. It is the place a fan opens during a match to choose a game,
understand what is happening, watch the play unfold, and mess around with
friends and the crowd while it happens.

The simplest version of the idea:

> **Pick a match. Follow the pulse. Watch the play. Bet your Lambo (playfully).**

## Why it exists

Most fans already use a second screen during football. They check the score,
scroll chat, hunt for key moments, and try to understand what changed in the
match. Those experiences are split across too many places:

- score apps show the facts but feel passive
- chats are social but unstructured
- feeds are noisy and detached from the live match
- betting products use the match as a market, not a fan experience

GameCrew combines the useful parts without becoming any of those things. It
should feel like **a match poster, a live timeline, a stylized broadcast, and
a rowdy group of friends** in one mobile flow.

## The four pillars

1. **Match Pulse** — a grounded live commentary timeline, wired to real match
   data. [Follow the story →](/journey/match-pulse/)
2. **Game View** — a top-down animated visualization of the play, driven by
   live semantic frames. [Watch the play →](/journey/game-view/)
3. **Global Chat** — one global room per match. Everyone watching that game is
   in it. [Join the crowd →](/journey/global-chat/)
4. **Playful Economy** — free gifts staked on real match moments, with
   coolness points that only ever go up. Assets live on Solana, and no real
   money is ever involved. [Bet your Lambo →](/journey/playful-economy/)

## Grounded in real data

Everything in GameCrew traces back to **TxLINE live match data**. The
commentary timeline, the animated board, and the settlement of every playful
call are all driven by real match events — not scripts, not randomness.

Built for the **TxODDS World Cup hackathon**.
